About Us


Kamla Nehru Sanghralaya is a treasure trove of history, culture, and heritage, dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich legacy of our past. Named in honor of Kamla Nehru, a prominent figure in India's freedom struggle and a symbol of resilience, the museum serves as a beacon of knowledge and inspiration for generations to come.
Situated in INDORE, Kamla Nehru Sanghralaya was established with the vision of bridging the gap between history and the present. Our collections are a vibrant blend of artifacts, artworks, manuscripts, and cultural relics that reflect the diverse and intricate fabric of our society. Our Mission
At Kamla Nehru Sanghralaya, we aim to:
  • Encouraging Preservation: Promoting awareness about the importance
    of safeguarding our tangible and intangible heritage for future generations.
  • Preserve our historical and cultural heritage for future generations.
  • Educate visitors about the rich history and traditions that define our identity.
  • Inspire curiosity, dialogue, and deeper connections with our shared heritage.

What We Offer

  • Permanent Exhibits showcasing rare artifacts and historical memorabilia.

  • Educational Programs for schools, colleges, and enthusiasts to promote learning through interactive workshops and
