Our Services

At Kamla Nehru Sanghralaya, we aim to provide a seamless and enriching experience for our visitors. From exploring our exhibits to engaging in educational programs, our range of services ensures a memorable visit for everyone.
Explore Our Services
1. Online Ticket Booking
We understand the importance of convenience in today's fast-paced world. To make your visit hassle-free, we offer an online ticket booking facility.
Save Time: Skip the queues by purchasing your tickets online.
Secure Payments: Enjoy safe and easy payment options through our online portal.
Plan Ahead: Choose your preferred date and time for a smooth visit experience.
Visit our Online Ticket Portal to book your tickets now!
2. Guided Tours
Enhance your experience with our curated guided tours led by knowledgeable experts. Learn the stories behind our exhibits and gain deeper insights into the treasures on display.
Group and individual tours are available.
Advance booking is recommended.
3. Educational Workshops
We host interactive workshops for students, educators, and heritage enthusiasts. These sessions are designed to foster creativity, learning, and an appreciation for history and culture.
4. Event Spaces
Looking for a unique venue for your next event? Our museum offers dedicated spaces for exhibitions, cultural events, and educational programs. Contact us to learn more about availability and booking options.
5. Accessibility Services
We strive to ensure an inclusive experience for all our visitors:
Wheelchair accessibility. Audio guides for enhanced engagement. Assistance for visitors with special needs.
