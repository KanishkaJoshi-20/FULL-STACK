Welcome to Kamla Nehru Indore Zoo
**A Journey into Nature and Wildlife Conservation
Nestled in the heart of Indore, Kamla Nehru Zoo is more than just a zoo—it's an oasis where nature meets education and adventure. Established in 1974, this iconic destination is home to an incredible variety of animals, birds, and reptiles, offering visitors of all ages an unforgettable experience.

**Explore Our Wildlife
Discover the fascinating world of wildlife through our diverse collection of species, including majestic tigers, playful primates, colorful exotic birds, and more. The zoo is dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing habitat for over 200 animals, ensuring their health and well-being.
**A Mission of Conservation
At Kamla Nehru Zoo, we’re committed to wildlife conservation and education. Our programs aim to inspire visitors to learn about biodiversity and understand the importance of protecting the natural world.
**What Awaits You?
- **Animal Encounters:** Meet a wide variety of species from around the globe.
- **Reptile House:** Get up close with fascinating reptiles and amphibians.
- **Children's Park:** A fun and interactive space for kids to enjoy.
- **Nature Walks:** Explore the beauty of nature while walking through lush green surroundings.
**Plan Your Visit
Whether you’re planning a family outing, a school trip, or just a day of exploration, Kamla Nehru Zoo offers something for everyone. Come and enjoy a day filled with adventure, education, and the beauty of wildlife.
